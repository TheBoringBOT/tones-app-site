Privacy Policy


We don't store your personal data

Our app doesn't collect or store any of your personal data. All data is stored locally on your device, and we don't have access to it.


Anonymous Data Collection

While we do not store personal information, we track anonymous events within the app to improve user experience and functionality. These events are collected without any personally identifiable information and help us understand how users interact with the app.

Tracked Anonymous Events:

  • Audio Start and Finish: We track when audio sessions are started and finished, including the preset ID and session duration.
  • Favorite Preset: We track when a user favorites or unfavorites a preset.
  • Mixer Modifications: We track changes to the audio mixer, such as volume adjustments, reverb settings, tone volume, noise volume, and equalizer type changes.
  • Default Timer Usage: We track when the default timer is used.
  • Preferred Timer Usage: We track when a user selects or uses a preferred timer duration.
  • Refocus Sounds: We track when users select, start, or end refocus sounds, and the chosen refocus sound. We also track changes to refocus sound intervals.
  • Screen Settings: We track when the "Keep Screen Awake" setting is toggled.
  • Mix with Other Apps: We track when the "Mix with Other Apps" setting is toggled.
  • Restart Timer: We track when the timer is reset or restarted.

These events are collected to help us optimize the app's functionality, improve features, and deliver a better experience. All tracked data is anonymous and cannot be linked to any specific user.


Analytics Tools

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous data. Firebase Analytics helps us understand how users engage with our app and allows us to improve features based on usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected through Firebase Analytics.


Privacy Policy Changes

Although most changes are likely to be minor, we may change our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage visitors to frequently check this page for any changes to our Privacy Policy.
